Last Updated: 23/08/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Video Manager App ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • TikTok account information used to upload videos.
  • Video metadata, such as video IDs, titles, and hashtags.
  • Logs related to app usage to prevent duplicate uploads.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide the functionality of the app, including:

  • Uploading videos to your TikTok account.
  • Organizing and managing uploaded content.
  • Maintaining logs to avoid duplicate uploads.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your TikTok account information is used solely for uploading videos via the app.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your TikTok account credentials safe.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate the app, but they are bound to handle your information securely.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
